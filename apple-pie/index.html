Apple Pie

This was my grandmother's apple pie recipe. I have never seen another one quite like it. It will always be my favorite and has won me several first-place prizes in local competitions.

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste...
  2. Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice crust...
  3. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake 35–45 more minutes...
